Burger & fries

This Classic Beef Burger with a side of fries is a perfect blend of juicy beef, melted cheese, and fresh toppings, all encased in a soft brioche bun, creating a timeless and delicious meal that's sure to satisfy any burger lover.

Preparation Time

  • Total: Aproximately 15 minutes
  • Cooking: 12 minues

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef (80/20 blend recommended for optimal juiciness)
  • Freshly ground black pepper (to taste) and salt
  • 4 brioche buns (or any preferred type of burger bun)
  • 4 slices of cheddar cheese (or any preferred type of cheese like Swiss or American)
  • Toppings of your choice

Instructions

1

Season the ground beef with salt, pepper, and any additional seasonings (garlic powder, Worcestershire sauce).

2

Cook the patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side for medium doneness (adjust time for preferred doneness).

3

Place a slice of cheese on each patty during the last minute of cooking to melt.
